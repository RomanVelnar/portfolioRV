Building Web portfolios

Starting with my software development career, I was looking to do some external projects. Many people were complaining that it was difficult to find a job during the Covid-19 quarantine.

My plan was to build them a web portfolio that would increase their chances to find a job. Building these websites also improved my skills as a developer and web designer.

The websites are build with HTML, CSS and vanilla JavaScript. I tried to avoid any JS libraries just to focus on the fundamentals.
